Welcome to our Louisiana Adventures page!

Please be patient while we build our site.

What can I say about Louisiana...it's home. I'm sure when many people think about the state they often picture New Orleans and the French Quarter or possibly Mardi Gras. For us though it's much different. It's backwoods living, it's downtown Baton Rouge, it's swamps and aligators, it's being stationed at Fort Polk.
Some good times in LA often just include time with family...for us anyway. Honestly growing up in the state there isn't much in which to get exicted. Fond memories include campfires, crawfish boils, visits to New Orleans, campling, hiking, going to the lake. It may not seem like much for some people but those are the times that cement in your memory, times where there aren't a lot of distractions, just you and the people you love. Smoking, joking, and BSing.
