Welcome to the How To Page!

A place where we share some advice for our hobbies.



Hobbies:
Because we have such a large family our hobbies are diverse. My wife loves to read, two of my daughters love art and drawing, most of my daughters love anime, my son loves gaming, and I enjoy woodworking. Our common hobby is absolutely traveling. Here, I'll share some advice on my hobby (woodworking) as well as traveling. Over time I hope to rope in the family so they can share what they love as well.

Woodworking:
I love woodworking, it's pleasurable to see something through to to finish then be able to make great use out of it. It's even a better feeling when I'm able to use re-claimed wood. One of my earliest projects of using reclaimed wood from pallets was to build a wheel-burrow style planter for my wife. Check out this video from Steve Ramsey's YouTube channel "Wood Working for Mere Mortals".
