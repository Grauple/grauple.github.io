Vegas:

18 Years, that's how long it took before Sharon and I got to take a vacation without children. We've always been under the impression that we should be making memories WITH the kids but any parent can tell you that a vacation with the kids isn't exactly a vacation for the adults. Yes it can be fun but it's hardly relaxing.
In November of 2021 we embarked on our first "no kids" vacation. We were able to leave the kids with their Grandmother so we could finally focus on enjoying one another. We had an absolute blast and were able to stoke the flames of our relationship. Since then we've decided to make our couple's vacation an annual event.
