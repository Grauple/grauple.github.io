Sound Waves:

Begin by watching this video of a sound wave experiment. Be prepared to discuss, answer questions, and write about what you've learned: How does sound travel, how can we help sound propogate, why did it take 11 seconds for the sound to reach their third spot? After watching the video feel free to use any resource to assist with answering these questions. Ensure you're work is performed at the minimum required for your grade level. Let me know if you have questions.

Curiosity Stream: Black Holes

Watch the video about observing black wholes. Be prepared to have an opend discussion as a group on what you've learned. Some items to consider: What are black wholes, how is it possible to observe them, why is it difficult to observe them?
